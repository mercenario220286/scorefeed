What is ScoreFeed?

ScoreFeed is a football-first oracle that publishes verified match results on BNB Chain. We focus on top-tier leagues and clean, structured data – starting with the Premier League and La Liga.

Football-native

Scores, scorers, goal minutes and cards packaged for Web3 builders, not just human viewers.

Oracle-ready

Data is fetched from multiple trusted providers, cross-checked and normalized before being pushed on-chain.

Built on BNB Chain

Low fees and EVM compatibility, ideal for prediction markets, betting dApps and AI agents that need reliable football data.

How it works

1

Collect

We fetch match data from multiple trusted football data providers for the leagues we cover.

2

Validate

Our engine cross-checks sources, cleans player names and normalizes events into a stable schema.

3

Publish

Final, verified match results are published to the ScoreFeed oracle contract on BNB Chain and exposed via API.

Roadmap

ScoreFeed is starting focused and lean, then expanding to more leagues, features and integrations.

Phase 1 – Core oracle

Static site, brand and public app live on Vercel. Premier League & La Liga defined as the initial focus with a clear data model for scores, scorers, minutes and cards.

Phase 2 – More leagues

Add Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1 and MLS. Expand coverage while keeping one consistent data schema.

Phase 3 – APIs & tools

Public HTTP API, SDKs and dashboards for builders. Match history, on-chain links and developer analytics.

Phase 4 – Ecosystem

Dedicated plans for prediction markets, bookmakers, fantasy platforms and AI projects that need football data with on-chain guarantees.
