What is ScoreFeed?

ScoreFeed is a football-first oracle that publishes verified match results on BNB Chain. We focus on top-tier leagues and clean, structured data – starting with the Premier League and La Liga.

Football-native

Scores, scorers, goal minutes and cards packaged for Web3 builders, not just human viewers.

Oracle-ready

Data is fetched from multiple trusted providers, cross-checked and normalised before being pushed on-chain.

Built on BNB Chain

Low fees and EVM compatibility, ideal for prediction markets, betting dApps and AI agents that need reliable football data.

How it works

1

Collect

We fetch match data from multiple trusted football data providers for Premier League and La Liga.

2

Validate

Our AI engine cross-checks sources, cleans player names and normalises events in near real time.

3

Publish

Final, verified match results are published to the ScoreFeed oracle contract on BNB and exposed via API.

Roadmap

Phase 1 – MVP online

Static site, brand and landing page live on Vercel. Premier League & La Liga defined as initial focus, with clear data model for scores, scorers, minutes and cards.

Phase 2 – Oracle engine

Backend connects to multiple football data providers. AI layer validates events and prepares final results ready to be pushed on BNB.

Phase 3 – On-chain & dashboard

ScoreFeed oracle contract deployed on BNB mainnet. Matches published automatically. Public dashboard lists fixtures and results, with links to on-chain data.

Phase 4 – More leagues & integrations

Add Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1 and MLS. Open up API access and paid plans for prediction markets, dApps and AI agents.
