What is ScoreFeed?

ScoreFeed is a football-first oracle that publishes verified match results on BNB Chain. We focus on top-tier leagues only, starting with Premier League and La Liga.

Football-native

Scores, scorers, goal minutes and cards – packaged for Web3 builders, not just human viewers.

AI-verified

Data is fetched from multiple providers and validated with AI before being pushed on-chain.

Built on BNB

Low gas, EVM compatible and ready for prediction markets, dApps and AI agents.

How it works

1

Collect

We fetch match data from multiple trusted football data providers for Premier League and La Liga.

2

Validate

Our AI engine cross-checks sources, cleans player names and normalises event minutes.

3

Publish

Final, verified match results are published to the ScoreFeed oracle contract on BNB and exposed via API.

Roadmap

ScoreFeed is starting small and focused, then expanding to more leagues, features and integrations. This is the current MVP roadmap.

Phase 1 – MVP Online

Static site, brand and landing page live on Vercel. Premier League & La Liga defined as initial focus, with clear data model for scores, scorers, minutes and cards.

Phase 2 – Oracle Engine

Backend connects to multiple football data providers. AI layer validates events and prepares final results ready to be pushed on-chain on BNB testnet.

Phase 3 – On-Chain & Dashboard

ScoreFeed oracle contract deployed on BNB mainnet. Matches published automatically. Public dashboard lists fixtures and results, with links to on-chain data.

Phase 4 – More Leagues & Integrations

Add Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1 and MLS. Open up API access and paid plans for prediction markets, dApps and AI agents that need reliable football data.
